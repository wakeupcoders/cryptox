Get to know about our pacca artisans project

We are developing a system for artisans (mainly handicrafts) with following features through blockchain.

1. Traceability of original artisan.
2. No need for big auction galleries.
3. Interconnecting artisans and art lovers.
4. Maintaining art lover anonymity.
5. Developing digitised art work from artisan’s physical work.
6. Ownership rights to art lovers.
7. Certification of high-quality digital format.
8. Transaction through cryptocurrency to reduce intermediaries’ commission.
9. Promoting and setting up online, autonomous and decentralised marketplaces.

Let's have a look at our pacca farmer project

We are working on a project where-in farmers can sell directly to consumers. This system will allow to trace back supply chain by consumers and even farmers can trace how their produce managed and processed.

1. Main deliverables are as follows.
2. Real-time monitoring.
3. Quick decision making (by farmers, consumers and processors).
4. Organised inventory system.
5. Blockchain of farmers, consumers and processors (no one can dominate).
6. Automation.
7. Reduction of labour cost.
